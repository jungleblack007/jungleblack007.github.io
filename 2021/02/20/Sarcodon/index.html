肉齿菌属 Sarcodon

小黑Black_007
蘑菇分类

发布于：2021年2月20日
次浏览

​ 肉齿菌属Sarcodon在国内目前报道了8个物种，属内的物种常被叫做“虎掌菌”。

烟白齿菌科 Bankeraceae

肉齿菌属 Sarcodon

1. Sarcodon coactus

产地：云南楚雄紫溪山，云南马关

原文描述：S. coactus is characterised by ellipsoid to round basidiocarps, reddish-brown to dark brown, felted pileal surface with white and incurved margins, simple-septate and partly short-celled generative hyphae and irregular subglobose, thin-walled, brown ba- sidiospores with tuberculate ornamentation (tuberculi up to 1 μm long).

机翻：S. coactus的特征是椭球形到圆形的子实体，红棕色到深棕色，毛毡状的绒毛表面具白色和弯曲的边缘，单隔膜和部分短细胞的生成菌丝，以及不规则的近球状，薄壁的棕色芽胞 结节装饰物（结节长达1μm）

图片来源：https:// doi.org/10.3897/mycokeys.66.49910

2. Sarcodon grosselepidotus

产地：云南楚雄紫溪山，

原文描述：S. grosselepidotus is characterised by infundibuliform to round, occasionally deeply fissured pileus, pale orange to dark ruby pileal surface with ascending and coarse scales, simple-septate generative hyphae and irregular ellipsoid to globose, thin-walled, brown basidiospores with tuberculate ornamentation (tuberculi up to 0.7 μm long).

机翻：S. grosselepidotus的特征是漏斗形到圆形，偶有深裂的绒毛，浅橙色到暗红宝石的绒毛表面，具上升的和粗糙的鳞片，单隔生成的菌丝和不规则的椭圆形到球状，薄壁的棕色带孢子的结节孢子（结节状长达0.7μm）。

图片来源：https:// doi.org/10.3897/mycokeys.66.49910

3. Sarcodon lidongensis

产地：云南省怒江傈僳族自治州兰坪白族普米族自治县

原文描述：S. lidongensis is characterised by plano-convex to somewhat depressed and regular orbicular pileus, light brown to dark brown pileal surface with adhering squamose and purplish-brown, incurved and occasionally incised margin, cylindrical or broadened below stipe, simple-septate generative hyphae and irregular ellipsoid to subglobose, thin-walled basidiospores with tuberculate ornamentation (tuberculi up to 1 μm long).

机翻：S. lidongensis的特征是平凸到有点凹陷和规则的圆形绒毛，浅棕色到深棕色绒毛表面，有粘附的鳞片和紫褐色，弯曲且偶尔切开边缘，圆柱状或在柄下方变宽，单隔生生菌丝和不规则椭圆形亚球状，壁薄的担子孢子，具结节状装饰（结节长至1μm）。

图片来源：https:// doi.org/10.3897/mycokeys.66.49910
